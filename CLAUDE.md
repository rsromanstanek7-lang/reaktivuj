# YouTube Mastery — Email Sequence Project

This project manages email marketing for **Roman Staněk's YouTube business** — YouTube Mastery.

Roman teaches Czech/Slovak audience how to build faceless, outsourced YouTube channels that generate passive income. He is a digital nomad who has lived in 70+ countries.

## Brand
- **Brand:** YouTube Mastery
- **Founder:** Roman Staněk
- **Tone:** Informal tykání, story-first, personal, specific numbers
- **Signature:** „Měj se skvěle, Roman / YouTube Mastery · ytmastery.cz/ebook"
- **ESP:** ConvertKit ($39/měsíc) — Konverzky nepodporuje custom HTML
- **Funnel:** eBook (free) → Trénink zdarma (VSL) → Paid course (1 997 Kč)
- **VSL/CTA URL:** `https://ytmastery.cz/ebook/upsell-351925`

## Key Rules
1. Always tykání — never vykání
2. Story opens every email — specific, personal, real
3. ❌/✅ failure/win structure when listing
4. Real numbers always (477 dolarů, 1 997 Kč, 3 dolary/video)
5. PS teaser at end of every email
6. One CTA per email — ALWAYS to "trénink zdarma", NEVER direct course sale
7. NEVER put price in subject line
8. Subject style: lowercase, specific numbers, story moments
9. Income numbers: 1 000 videí = $300–400/měsíc
